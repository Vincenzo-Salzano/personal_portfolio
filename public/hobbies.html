About me and my free time

Sport

Among my passions, there is special place for body training. I really enjoy hitting the gym, but most of all, I really like to study and apply everything is behind muscle building and the discipline of Bodybuilding.

I have also a background of 9 years in swimming, but I have to say that I'm happy as well whenever there is the chance for trekking, or just long walks in the nature!

Traveling

Since I was very young, I have always been passionate about travels and exploration of foreign Countries. I thing that knowing about other culture, customs and tradition is one of the most exciting and fascinating aspects of any travel.

So, whenever I can, I grab my backpack and head to a new destination, aiming not only to visit the most 'touristy' places but also to connect with the true essence of the location.

Cinema, history but also...videogames!

I'm passionate about cinema and TV series in general, with Christopher Nolan being one of my favorite directors. His films always leave me fascinated with their captivating storytelling and unique visual style.

I'm deeply interested in history as well, from ancient Rome and Greece to modern Italian history. Exploring the rise and fall of empires, the achievements and challenges of leaders, and how societies change over time has always fascinated me and sparked my curiosity for learning.

Last but not least, I enjoy videogames, which provide both fun and intellectual challenges. I especially like strategy games, where every choice matters, and winning requires careful planning and skillful tactics.
